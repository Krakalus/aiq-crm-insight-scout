# Grok 3 API Compliance Checklist for Quantum Dynamics Inc.

**Purpose**: Ensure adherence to key US laws for CRM AI integration. Review quarterly.

## CCPA/CPRA Compliance
- [ ] Implement "Do Not Sell My Personal Information" opt-out for California residents.
- [ ] Maintain records of data processing activities for 24 months.
- [ ] Conduct data protection impact assessments for high-risk processing.

## Federal Trade Commission (FTC) Act
- [ ] Avoid deceptive practices in AI-generated customer responses.
- [ ] Disclose AI usage in CRM interactions per FTC guidelines on transparency.

## Export Controls (EAR/ITAR)
- [ ] Verify end-users not on US denied parties list (BIS Entity List).
- [ ] No export of technical data without license if dual-use.

## Accessibility (ADA/Section 508)
- [ ] Ensure API outputs are compatible with screen readers for CRM accessibility.

## Audit Rights
Quantum Dynamics Inc. may audit xAI's compliance annually, with 30 days' written notice.
